One day, Mia painted a majestic dragon, and to her surprise, it sprang to life and soared into the sky. She giggled with delight as the dragon playfully flew around her. Mia spent days painting all sorts of creatures and objects, and soon her village was filled with the wonders of her magical art.

However, one evening, Mia's paintbrush disappeared. She searched high and low, but it was nowhere to be found. Feeling sad and lost, Mia went to the forest and sat beneath a giant oak tree.
